Health Information

Explore essential health information and tips to maintain your well-being and prevent diseases.

Heart Health

Maintain a healthy heart by eating a balanced diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding tobacco.

Physical Activity

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity each week.

Mental Wellness

Practice mindfulness, get adequate sleep, and maintain social connections to support mental health.

Nutrition

Eat a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats for optimal health.

Medication Management

Follow prescriptions exactly as directed and keep regular appointments with healthcare providers.

Fitness

Incorporate strength training at least twice a week along with cardiovascular exercise.
